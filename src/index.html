1. Invest R6 000

Using your YunaCoin buy a cow for as little as R6 000! We accept payments in Ether allowing you to invest in a cow in South Africa from anywhere in the world!

2. Choose a Farm

We allow you to choose a cow that you like! The money you invest will go to the living expenses of the cow! Yes, living expenses! The money will help nuture and grow the cow up until it's ready to be go into the world!

3. Track your Investment

We allow you to get even closer to your investment! Track the daily movements and growth of your cow! Using Internet of Things (IOT) technology, you will always be kept in the loop!

4. Have Patience

Yes this was starting to sound too good to be true... There is a waiting period. Unfortunately we can't rush nature. Once you invest in a cow, you have to wait 6 months minimum to get your money back, with interest of course!

5. Reach Maturity

When your cow reaches maturity, it will be sold to an arbitour! That is the end of the investment plan. This usually happens after 9-12 months. You will be notified sooner to the time.

6. Receive Returns

You will receive your initial investment plus interest! Interest ranges between 10-15% dependent on the market at the time.
